uint_to_bits

in std::conv 

fn uint_to_bits<#uint N>(input: uint<N>) -> [bool; N];

Interpret a uint as the raw bit values. output[0] is the LSB of the integer and output[N-1] is the MSB

Note that this means that 0b1100 will be [false, false, true, true] since arrays are written LSB first, while integers are written MSB first
